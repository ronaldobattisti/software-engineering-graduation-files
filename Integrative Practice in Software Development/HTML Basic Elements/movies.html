Django Unchained

Django Unchained tells the story of Django, a freed slave, who partners with bounty hunter Dr. King Schultz to rescue his wife, Broomhilda, from the cruel plantation owner Calvin Candie. After infiltrating Candie's estate under the guise of purchasing a fighter, tensions explode when Schultz kills Candie, sacrificing himself. Django avenges Schultz, massacres Candie's men, and destroys the plantation in a fiery climax. The film ends with Django and Broomhilda riding off together, free at last.
